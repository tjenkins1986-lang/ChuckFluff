# Chuck Fluff – Fly Fishing Journal

A personal fly fishing log for iOS, built for anglers who care about the details.

Chuck Fluff lets you record every session — location, weather, conditions, gear, and every fish you land — then review your history, spot patterns, and build a lifetime record of your time on the water.

---

## Features

- **Session logging** — date, location, duration, weather, wind, temperature, and notes
- **GPS location pinning** — drop a precise pin or use your current location
- **Catch recording** — species, weight, quantity, and method per fish
- **Gear tracking** — log the rod and reel used each session
- **Photo attachments** — add photos to any session
- **History view** — browse, sort, and edit all past sessions
- **Map view** — see every pinned fishing location on a map
- **Dashboard** — stats, species breakdown, and catch trends over time
- **Config** — manage your own species list, rods, and reels

---

## Privacy

Chuck Fluff does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No account is required. No analytics. No ads.

**Permissions used:**
- **Location** — used to pin your fishing spot on the map. Never stored remotely.
- **Photos** — used to attach images to sessions. Photos stay on your device.

## FAQ

**Does Chuck Fluff require an internet connection?**
No. The app works entirely offline. No internet connection is ever required.

**Where is my data stored?**
All data is stored locally on your device. It is not backed up to any external server. We recommend using iCloud device backup to protect your data.

**Can I export my data?**
Export functionality is not available in the current version. It is on the roadmap for a future release.

**How do I reset the app?**
Deleting and reinstalling the app will clear all data. This cannot be undone.

**Why does the app ask for location permission?**
Location is used solely to pin your fishing spot on the map within the app. Your location is never transmitted or stored anywhere outside your device.

**Why does the app ask for photo permission?**
Photos are used solely to attach images to your fishing sessions. They are stored locally on your device only.
